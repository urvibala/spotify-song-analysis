# Spotify Song Analysis

## Introduction
This project aims to explore streaming trends and factors influencing song popularity using the Spotify dataset analysis and Python. The dataset used in this analysis can be found [here](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/julianoorlandi/spotify-top-songs-and-audio-features/code).

## Questions Explored
1. What are the most popular artists in terms of streams?
2. What is the average duration of songs on Spotify?
3. Is there a correlation between the danceability and energy of a song?
4. Is there a correlation between energy and loudness of tracks?
5. Are tracks becoming more or less popular as they stay on the chart for longer?
6. What are the top 10 tracks with the highest tempo?
7. How many tracks are in major mode versus minor mode?
8. What are the top 10 tracks with the highest number of streams?
9. What are the top 10 Sources with highest Number of Tracks?
10. Which artists have the most tracks in the dataset?
11. What are the common characteristics shared by Taylor Swift's most popular tracks?
12. What factors contribute the most to a song being streamed more?

## Dataset
The dataset used for this analysis contains information about top songs and audio features. It includes various attributes such as artist name, track name, duration, danceability, energy, loudness, popularity, tempo, mode, and more.

## Methodology
The analysis is conducted using Python programming language along with libraries such as pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, and numpy for data manipulation, visualization, and analysis.

## Conclusion
The analysis aims to provide insights into streaming trends and factors influencing song popularity on Spotify. By addressing the questions explored, we aim to gain a better understanding of the dynamics of music streaming platforms and the factors that contribute to the success of songs.
